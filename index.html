Impact of the scattering representation \( S_J(X) \) on the reconstruction

To study the impact of the scattering representation \(S_J(X)\) on the reconstruction, we consider the ablation of the second-order terms in this representation. Because of the temporal averaging with \(\phi_J\), this ablation is expected to yield reconstruction of lower quality. This is demonstrated quantitatively and qualitatively on the TIMIT and Beethoven datasets.
